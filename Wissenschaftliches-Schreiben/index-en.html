The Anatomy of Digital Unfreedom

Digital Addiction: Mechanisms of Addiction in Social Media - In this module on scientific research, the challenge was to examine the addictive mechanisms of social media from both academic and journalistic perspectives.

The project involves a thorough analysis of neurophysiology (dopamine cycles, prefrontal cortex) and Persuasive System Design (PSD) according to academic standards in the G06 A1 paper. In a second step, these findings were translated into a journalistic article in the „ZEIT Wissen“-style. The focus here was on professional layout, emotional storytelling, and the clear, accessible presentation of complex technical concepts such as “variable rewards” or “infinite scrolling” for a broad readership.
