RAUMLUFT - AI in Brand Development

From idea to brand in a digital workflow: The focus of this project was the conceptualization and visual design of the fictional interior brand “RAUMLUFT.” The challenge was to bridge the gap between technical necessity (air purification) and aesthetic living standards (Organic Luxury/Invisible Tech).

The main emphasis was on evaluating AI tools in the creative process. While AI served as a strategic sparring partner for ideation and high-resolution prototyping (rendering furniture using integrated filter technology), the professional composition, the vectorization of the logo, and the final layout (Affinity Suite) remained in human hands.

Update: The visual works (posters) created as part of this project will be on public display throughout May 2026 at the Zweibrücken City Museum.
